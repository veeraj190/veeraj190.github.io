What to Expect?

Students will take a blend of online and in-person learning sessions that includes...

2 College Courses

- CS 135 (Computer Science I) - 3 Credits

- EDW 135 (Employment Skills) - 3 Credits

3 IT Workshop Series

- Web Design and Internet Computing

- Cybersecurity

- Cloud Computing

6 Industry Certification Exam Series

- AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner

- CompTIA IT Fundamentals (ITF+)

- CompTIA A+

- CompTIA Cloud+

- CompTIA Network+

- CompTIA Security+

*Upon completion, students will earn one of the following industry-recognized credentials based on their choosing. Information will be prepared by a guest speaker.

Summer Paid Internship

The industry partners will pay for up to 10 weeks of summer internship at $15+ per hour and guarantee job interviews for all students who complete the program.
